### Discovery Call Framework
- **Open:** "Before I show you anything, tell me — what's the #1 thing that would make this worth your time today?"
- **Qualify:** Budget? Timeline? Decision maker? Current pain?
- **Diagnose:** Where are leads coming from? What happens after they come in? What's the follow-up process?
- **Demo:** Show the AI agent doing exactly what solves their problem
- **Close:** "Based on what you've told me, the [tier] package fits. When do you want to get started?"

### Objection Handling

**"It's too expensive"**
→ "What's one missed lead worth to your business? A missed $5,000 client? This pays for itself in week one. What's holding you back beyond the number?"

**"I need to think about it"**
→ "Totally fair. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it budget, timing, or whether this will actually work?"

**"We already have someone doing this"**
→ "Great — are they available 24/7? What happens when a lead comes in at 11pm on a Sunday? Our AI never sleeps."

**"I'm not sure AI is right for us"**
→ "That's what most of our clients said before they saw their first 3am booking come in. Can I show you exactly what it looks like for a business like yours?"

**"I want to try it for free first"**
→ "We don't do free trials — we do paid pilots. The reason is the setup requires real work, and we only work with clients who are serious. I can offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the monthly, but not the setup."

### 6-Step Sales Follow-Up Sequence
- Day 1: "Just following up on our call — any questions I can answer?"
- Day 2: Value add — send a relevant case study or stat
- Day 3: Urgency — "We have one spot left this month for [their niche]"
- Day 5: Social proof — testimonial or client win
- Day 7: Direct ask — "Are you moving forward or should I close your file?"
- Day 10: Final — "I'm closing your file today. No hard feelings — let me know if timing changes"

### Pipeline Stages (ABC Company / Client Pipeline)
- New Lead → Proposal Sent → Proposal Accepted → Won / Closed
- Move opportunity stages based on conversation outcome
- Tag "new client" when Won to trigger onboarding sequence
