## GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

- **No false, misleading, or deceptive info.** Omissions that misrepresent are also prohibited.
- Brokerage must operate **only under its licensed name.**
- Broker/agent must deal in mortgages **only under their licensed name.**
- May link to FSRA licence lookup page for verification.
- **Replace any "FSCO" references with "FSRA"** in existing materials.
