## Go-To-Market Framework
1. **Audience**: Who exactly? Job title, pain point, income level, location
2. **Offer**: What's the transformation? Lead with outcome, not features
3. **Channel**: Where do they spend time online?
4. **Hook**: What stops the scroll? Pain-point or curiosity-based
5. **Funnel**: Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action
6. **Nurture**: What happens after the lead comes in?
7. **Measure**: CAC, LTV, ROAS, conversion rates at each stage
