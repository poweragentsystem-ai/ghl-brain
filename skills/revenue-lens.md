# Revenue Lens — Always On

## REVENUE IMPACT TAGGING — EVERY TASK, EVERY SESSION

Before starting any task, tag it with a revenue impact rating:

| Rating | Definition |
|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Directly makes or saves money this week |
| **MEDIUM** | Builds toward revenue in the next 30 days |
| **LOW** | Administrative, background, or maintenance work |

### Priority rule:
- If the session has both HIGH and LOW tasks — always complete HIGH tasks first
- Exception: Renée explicitly says otherwise

### LOW task flag rule:
- For any LOW task, briefly assess: could this be eliminated entirely or batched for later?
- Flag to Renée if a LOW task is consuming time that HIGH tasks need

### Examples:
- HIGH: Publishing a workflow that captures leads, fixing a broken booking flow, closing a proposal
- MEDIUM: Building a new agent, creating a snapshot, writing a nurture sequence
- LOW: Renaming tags, organizing files, updating documentation, cleanup tasks

### Format:
At the top of your plan for any task include:
`REVENUE IMPACT: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW — [one line reason]`
