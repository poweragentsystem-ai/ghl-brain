# Realtor Outbound — Mortgage Referral Partnership Play

**Created 2026-04-27 by Jordan while Renée slept.**
**Goal:** Cold-email/SMS realtors in GTA with a partnership offer where Renée handles their clients' mortgages. Each realtor closes 5-20 deals/year on average — even ONE active referral relationship = 3-5 deals/year × $4-8K commission = $12-40K.

**Why this is the highest-leverage outbound she has:**
- Realtors NEED a reliable mortgage partner (clients ask all the time)
- Renée is FSRA-licensed, OLS-brokered → real credibility
- Partnership is reciprocal (she sends buyer leads back when relevant)
- One yes = recurring deal flow, not one-off

**Hormozi take:** "The grand slam offer here isn't 'get me your clients.' It's 'I'll close their mortgage in 14 days, handle every doc, and keep YOU informed at every step so YOUR client thinks YOU made it happen.' You're not asking for leads. You're solving the realtor's biggest fear: a deal collapsing on financing."

## TARGETING — Who to email first

**Tier 1 — highest-leverage targets:**
- Realtors with **5-20 deals/year** (mid-volume — they're hungry but not over-served)
- Specializing in **first-time buyers** (most mortgage-dependent buyer profile)
- In **GTA fringes**: Mississauga, Brampton, Hamilton, Oakville, Burlington, Markham, Vaughan
- Active in last 90 days (recent listings)

**Tier 2:**
- Real estate teams (multi-agent shops) — one yes = whole team's pipeline
- Refi specialists (less common, but high-value when found)

**Avoid:**
- Top 1% (they have multiple brokers + dedicated relationships already)
- Brand-new agents (no deal flow yet)

**Where to find them (use existing lead-finder):**
- OSM: `office=estate_agent` + GTA cities (already in profile)
- Realtor.ca public agent directory (would need scraping)
- Local FB group: "GTA Realtors" / brokerage company pages
