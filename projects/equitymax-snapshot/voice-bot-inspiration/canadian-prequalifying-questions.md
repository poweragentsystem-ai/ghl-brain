# Canadian Mortgage Prequalifying Questions — Master Set

Source: Drive doc `1iXoP5Yq44jd4bSWqyjOrrvFLfCzEIOh-x7YVWGg-9lQ`. Pulled 2026-04-22.

These are the product-specific qualifying question sets for voice bots, chat bots, and forms. Any Canadian mortgage pro can use these as-is.

## Purchase
- Are you a first time buyer? [yes/no]
  - if yes → qualify at 95% LTV
  - if no → qualify at 80% LTV
- How soon are you looking to buy? [immediately / 1-3 mo / 3-6 mo / 6-9 mo / 9-12 mo / just browsing]
- What type of property? [condo / townhouse / detached / commercial]
- How many bedrooms?
- How many bathrooms?
- How much down payment do you have access to?
- What is your max purchasing budget? [if not sure, that's ok]
- Are you currently employed?
- What is your annual salary?
- What is the total of your debts? [credit cards, auto loans, student loans, child support, tax arrears]

## Refinance
- Are you a current property owner? [if no → ask if buying → use purchase path, otherwise does not qualify]
- Property type: condo / townhouse / detached / commercial?
- What city is your property located in?
- Refinance goal: lower rate / cover debts / other?
- Do you need access to cash? [if yes, how much?]
- How soon do you need financing? [timeframe options]
- Current mortgage balance?
- Credit card debt?
- Auto loans?
- Student loans?
- Tax arrears?
- Other debts on credit report?
- Estimated property value?

## HELOC / Private / Second
- Current property owner? [if no → purchase path or does not qualify]
- Property type?
- Property city?
- How much do you need access to?
- How soon do you need access to equity?
- Current mortgage balance?
- Credit card debt?
- Auto loans?
- Student loans?
- Tax arrears?
- Other debts?

## Reverse (55+)
- Are you 55+? [if no → offer refinance path, otherwise does not qualify]
- Current property owner? [if no → disqualify]
- Property type?
- Property city?
- How much do you need access to?
- Timeframe?
- Current mortgage balance?
- Credit card debt? Auto loans? Student loans? Tax arrears? Other debts?
- Estimated property value?

## Renewal
- Current property owner?
- Property type?
- Property city?
- When is mortgage up for renewal? [date]
- Current bank?
- Did they send a renewal offer letter?
- Terms (if renewal letter received): rate, term, fixed/variable
- Current mortgage balance?
- Just renew, or need access to cash? [if need cash for debts, don't double-count]
- Credit card debt? Auto loans? Student loans? Tax arrears? Other debts?
- Estimated property value?

## LTV Qualifying Rules
| Product | LTV Max | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Purchase (first-time) | under 95% | n/a |
| Purchase (other) | under 80% | n/a |
| Refinance / Second / Renewal / HELOC | under 80% | homeowner |
| Reverse | under 65% | homeowner + 55+ |

**Cross-sell rule:** if they qualify for another product, ask if interested (e.g. wants reverse but only 50 → offer refinance).

**Hard disqualifiers:**
- No personal loans — must own a home or be purchasing to qualify
- If they don't qualify, stop asking questions (no homeowner + not buying, or wants purchase with no down payment)
- Flag urgent cases (emergency funds needed + has equity)

## Wholesale (Real Estate Investor)
- Property type to wholesale? [condo / townhouse / detached / commercial]
- How soon need financing?
- City population at least 10,000? [must answer yes; if unknown, ask them to check]
- Property city?
- Purchase price?
- Down payment amount?
- Renovation budget?
- ARV (After Repair Value)?
- Renovation timeline (months)?
- Sell timeline after reno (months)?

## Special Wholesale Qualifying
**Rule:** Must project profit of at least $25K after sale to qualify for special financing.

**If does not qualify** → offer private mortgage (requires 20% down, max 80% LTV):
- "The numbers are tight for the low-downpayment wholesale based on my calculations, but we can possibly get you a regular private mortgage if you're interested."

### Wholesale Private Questions
- Access to 20% down payment? [if no → disqualify; private maxes at 80% LTV]
- Current property owner? [ok if no]
- Current mortgage balance? [only if property owner]
- Credit card debt? Auto loans? Student loans? Tax arrears? Other debts?
