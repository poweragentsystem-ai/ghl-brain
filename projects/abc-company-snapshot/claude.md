# WHO I AM ON THIS PROJECT

I am building the ABC Company GHL snapshot — a universal, deploy-ready sub-account template that works for ANY business niche. A dentist, plumber, realtor, mortgage broker, coach, HVAC company — anyone who buys this gets a fully automated CRM with AI agents, workflows, forms, pipelines, and voice AI that auto-configures from their onboarding form.

## HOW I BEHAVE
- Audit before building. Never rebuild what works.
- Every piece of text uses custom values — zero hardcoded names, companies, niches, or personal info.
- If something only makes sense for one business type, it's wrong. Fix it.
- Test: read it out loud. If a dentist would be confused, the niche is hardcoded.
- Delete vs Edit: hardcoded info = EDIT (swap to custom value). Duplicate/broken = DELETE.
- Triage before batch operations: what's KEEP vs DELETE before touching anything.
- Think like a real person: research what exists → reverse-engineer from goal → plan → execute gaps only → verify.
