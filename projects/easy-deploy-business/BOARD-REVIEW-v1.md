# Easy Deploy — Billion-Dollar Board Review (v1)

All 8 experts reviewed what Easy Deploy is right now (2026-04-23) and flagged what to fix/add/kill. Each expert gets a vote. Renée decides.

**Project state they reviewed:**
- Landing page + orb + create/demo + pipeline + leads + AI Team + inbox + my-business + funnel builder + dialer live at easy-deploy-app.vercel.app
- Demo auth (no backend yet)
- Pricing rough-in: $97/$197/$397 tiers + $0.10/min subscriber floor
- Marketing hook: "Build it. Own it. Ship it."
- Ulio is the named competitor

---

## HORMOZI · OFFER + PRICING

**Verdict: B-. The offer has bones but no teeth.**

Issues:
1. **No Grand Slam structure.** Pricing tiers exist but no guarantee, no scarcity, no named bonuses worth more than core. "Starter $97" is a SKU, not an offer.
2. **No Value Equation at surface.** Visitors see a price. They don't see dream outcome × likelihood divided by time × effort. They just see features.
3. **Guarantee missing.** High-ticket buyers need risk reversal. Ours = none.

Fixes:
- Name the flagship: not "Starter $97" → "The Recurring Revenue System: $97/mo"
- Guarantee: "If you don't book 5 qualified consults in your first 30 days, we rebuild your Orb for free AND extend your month."
- 3 bonuses with names that sound like products:
  - "The 500-Minute Launch Pack" (first-month minute bonus)
  - "The Niche Blueprint Library" (unlocks niche-specific templates)
  - "The First-Client Close Script" (Belfort-style closer for their first paying client)
- **Core before price:** landing page tells outcome + proof BEFORE showing the number

## CARDONE · SALES VOLUME

**Verdict: C. Your prospecting is invisible.**

Issues:
1. No visible outbound engine yet. You have a beautiful platform but nobody knows it exists.
2. Follow-up cadence is under-built. Cold Outreach skill exists but not activated.
3. Omnipresence is zero — no ads, no content, no SEO, no affiliates yet.

Fixes:
- 10X the outreach: 500 cold DMs/week + 100 cold calls/week starting day 1
- Sammy (your closer agent) runs a 12-touch follow-up on every cold prospect (not the 5 most agencies stop at)
- Omnipresence in 4 places minimum: IG · LinkedIn · TikTok · podcast appearances. Daily.
- Build the affiliate program early — Ulio's 17% lifetime is your lead magnet for creators who already post AI content

## BELFORT · SALES CLOSING

**Verdict: B+. Your closer scripts are good. Tonality is missing.**

What's working:
- `high-ticket-closer.md` has SPIN + Sandler + objection handling.
- Payment-on-call + signature-on-call rules in the skill (prevents "I'll think about it" death).

Issues:
- No tonality mapping. Every script needs 1 of Belfort's 9 tones annotated: *(certainty)* before the price, *(scarcity)* on the urgency line, *(hypothetical)* on future-pace.
- 3 Tens check isn't in the discovery flow. Agents book calls without confirming the prospect rates us 10/10 on product AND person AND company.

Fixes:
- Add tonality annotations to `high-ticket-closer.md` scripts
- Add 3 Tens scoring to the discovery framework — if prospect is <8/10 on any, raise certainty before closing
- Build a looping-objections sub-skill: every "No" auto-generates 3 intelligence questions before re-closing

## BRUNSON · FUNNELS

**Verdict: B. You built a funnel builder but haven't built your own funnel.**

Issues:
1. Your value ladder is implicit, not explicit. Free audit → $47 trial → $97 starter → $197 Grow → $397 Scale → Consulting ($3K-$15K) isn't visible anywhere.
2. You don't have a perfect webinar yet. That's the single highest-converting B2B SaaS funnel type for $1K+ buyers.
3. Soap opera sequence missing — no 5-day welcome email series.

Fixes:
- Map the visible value ladder on the public pricing page + in every proposal
- Record a 45-minute perfect webinar: "How I Built a $1M AI Agency with Claude Code" — gate behind Easy Deploy demo signup
- Build the 5-day soap opera via Gmail MCP (Day 1: story. Day 2: strategy. Day 3: stakes. Day 4: solution. Day 5: soft CTA.)

## CONSOLIDATED VERDICT

**A (Godin, Kennedy, Robbins):** positioning + premium + state are strong. Ownership hook is defensible.

**B (Hormozi, Belfort, Brunson):** strong bones, missing the stack-value / tonality / explicit ladder detail.

**C-D (Cardone, Gary Vee):** prospecting + content are invisible. This is the biggest gap.

**If Renée only fixes 3 things next:**
1. **Build the content engine** (Gary Vee) — daily posting starts now. Without attention, no matter how good the product is, nothing converts.
2. **Grand Slam Offer v2 on the pricing page** (Hormozi + Kennedy) — named bonuses + guarantee + risk reversal. Before any backend wiring.
3. **10X outbound activation** (Cardone) — 500 cold DMs/week starts Week 1. Sammy runs the 12-touch on every prospect.

Everything else is polish.
