## TARGETING — Who to email first

**Tier 1:**
- Mortgage agents in Ontario with 1-3 years license
- Active on social (signals they're trying to grow but probably bootstrapping)
- Independent or part of small brokerage (vs RBC/banks where they can't add tools)

**Tier 2:**
- Mortgage brokerage owners (sell to the SHOP, not the agent — multiplier)
- US LO equivalents (post-Canadian launch — bigger market, similar problem)

**Where to find:**
- FSRA Mortgage Broker Registry (PUBLIC government data — FREE, comprehensive Ontario list)
- LinkedIn (mortgage agent + GTA filters)
- Mortgage broker FB groups
